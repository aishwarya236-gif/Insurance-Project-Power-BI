## üìÇ Data Sources  
1.	brokerage.xlsx ‚Äì Contains comprehensive details of insurance policies, including policy numbers, client names, assigned agents, branch names, product groups, premium amounts, and renewal status.

5.	meeting.xlsx ‚Äì Logs all client meetings conducted by agents, including meeting dates, participating account executives, and related opportunity or policy context.  
6.	fees.xlsx ‚Äì Provides information on client names, assigned agents, solution groups, and income class for fee-based services.  
7.	Policy Data.xlsx ‚Äì Consolidates policy-related information including policy details, payment history, customer demographics, and claim records.

## üß© Branch Dashboard KPIs  
1.	Number of Invoices by Account Executive  
‚Ä¢	Definition: Total invoices generated by each Account Executive.  
‚Ä¢	Purpose: Tracks revenue responsibility and individual performance.  
2.	Yearly Meeting Count  
‚Ä¢	Definition: Number of client meetings held each year.  
‚Ä¢	Purpose: Measures customer engagement and sales effort.  
3.	Target vs Achieved (New, Cross-sell, Renewal, Invoice)  
‚Ä¢	Definition: Compares actual performance against defined targets for each business type.  
‚Ä¢	Purpose: Evaluates branch productivity and goal completion.  
4.	Stage Funnel by Revenue  
‚Ä¢	Definition: Revenue contribution across stages.  
‚Ä¢	Purpose: Visualizes the opportunity pipeline and conversion efficiency.  
5.	Number of Meetings by Account Executive  
‚Ä¢	Definition: Meetings categorized by Account Executive.  
‚Ä¢	Purpose: Tracks engagement per executive to identify high-performing relationship managers.  
6.	Top Open Opportunities  
‚Ä¢	Definition: List of top potential deals not yet closed, sorted by revenue amount.  
‚Ä¢	Purpose: Helps focus on high-value pending opportunities.

## üßæ Policy Dashboard KPIs  
1.	Total Policies  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Total count of all policies (active + inactive).  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Measures overall policy portfolio size.  
2.	Total Customers  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Distinct count of clients who hold one or more policies.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Identifies customer base and potential for cross-selling.  
3.	Age Bucket Wise Policy Count   
‚Ä¢  Definition: Policies grouped by customer age ranges.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Reveals which age segments dominate the portfolio.  
4.	Gender Wise Policy Count   
‚Ä¢  Definition: Policies grouped by gender.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Helps analyze market penetration by gender.  
5.	Policy Type Wise Policy Count  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Number of policies distributed by policy type.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Identifies best-selling insurance categories.  
7.	Policies Expiring This Year  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Count of policies set to expire within the current calendar year.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Tracks upcoming renewals for proactive follow-up.  
8.	Premium Growth Rate  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Percentage increase in premium revenue over a specific time period.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Measures premium revenue growth over time.  
9.	Claim Status Wise Policy Count  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Policies segmented by claim status.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Evaluates claims handling performance.  
10.	Payment Status Wise Policy Count   
‚Ä¢  Definition: Segmentation of policies based on payment completion.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Monitors financial compliance.  
11.	Total Claim Amount  
‚Ä¢  Definition: Total claims payout value.  
‚Ä¢  Purpose: Assesses the impact of claims on profitability.
